About Me

  • Software: I enjoy making useful products, meaning software that has a solid link with the real world. What I like the most is making libraries, as a means to promote collaboration among people. In fact, it made me kind of excel at refactoring.
  • VPN: After Private Internet Access, I strived to make a 100% Swift OpenVPN library for iOS/macOS (TunnelKit), that both providers and indie developers could quickly integrate in their apps. Passepartout was a showcase of that library and has grown in features every month since. Three years after the first beta, Passepartout is the app that many users prefer today over OpenVPN Connect and Tunnelblick.
